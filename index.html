Hey! Welcome to my Round Pen

This is where you get to know me a little better because I don't have a say in the matter :) (a bit of horse humor). I'm a former Marine Corps Officer. Served for a decade as an Aviator, I flew those helicopter/ plane combo things (MV-22B) for my career. I thoroughly enjoyed it, but I am now enjoying civilian life even more.

I decided I wanted to be able to work from home... got my MBA, and then randomly discovered software. I haven't looked back since. Surprisingly, I do find it just as (if not even more so) stimulating as flying. I love challenging my brain and cannot wait to see where I end up.

I'm a huge gear-head and tinkerer. I love making things work so I'm hoping I could land somewhere in the automotive or aviation sector.

I also volunteer at a ranch up the road everyday. I find the horses to be incredibly therapeutic and LOVE the fact that I can work on my own schedule, so long as I get the work done.
